The Seed Stitch

This page is to talk about a specific type of stitch in knitting. It is a simple beginnner stitch but it is one that can be used in many different applications and is great for any one who knits to have in their skill set.

Seed Stitch

The seed stitch is a simple variation on the core stitch of knitting, which is commonly referred to as a straigh stitich or a garter stitch. The only difference between the basic stitch and the seed stitch is the use of an alternating purl stitch as opposed to justing just a repating straight stitch. The alternation and subsequent grouping of yarn is then what creates the different texture, and gives any pattern a distinct look.
